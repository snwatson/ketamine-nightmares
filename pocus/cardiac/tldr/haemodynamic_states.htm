Haemodynamic states

 

LV systolic failure

*Flaccid LV*

·     ↑Systolic volume

·     ↓Diastolic volume

·     ↑LA size

LV diastolic failure

*Stiff LV*

·     ↓Diastolic volume

·     ↔Systolic volume

·     ↑LA size

RV systolic failure

*Flaccid RV*

*Empty LV*
*Dilated IVC*

·     ↑RV diastolic volume (and flattened septum)

·     ↑RV systolic volume

·     ↓LV diastolic volume

·     ↓LV systolic volume

·     ↑PASP (usual cause)

·     IVC dilated and non-collapsing

RV diastolic failure

*Potato*

Hypovolaemia

*Empty LV*

·     ↓Diastolic volume

·     ↓Systolic volume

·     IVC small and collapsing

Vasodilation

*Collapsing LV*

·     ↔LV end-diastolic volume

·     ↓LV end-systolic volume

Tamponade

*Swollen pericardium*

*Constricted heart*

*Respiratory oscillation*

·     Pericardial space >0.5cm (severe >2cm)

·     Cardiac chambers: collapsed

o   Systole before diastole

o   Right before left

o   Atria before ventricles

·     Inflow vessels:

o   Dilation

o   Diastolic flow reversal (pulmonary & hepatic)

·     Inspiratory changes:

o   ↓LV output (i.e. pulsus paradoxus)

HOCM

*LVOT obstruction*

·     LVH (septal)

·     SAM (severe = throughout systole)

·     MR (posterior)

·     LVOTO (dagger shape)
