Thoracic surgery – miscellaneous

 

Post-op complications:

Respiratory

·     T1RF (lung removal, atelectasis, oedema, secretions)

·     T2RF (as above, pain)

·     Pneumonia

Cardiovascular

·     Arrhythmias – AF in 30-50%

·     Ischaemia

·     PHTN / RV dysfunction (∝ lung removal)

 

Underwater drain:

 

(in the atrium system, a pop-off valve in the seal chamber performs the function of a safety bottle)

 

Problems:

No safety bottle

·     Build up of positive pressure

No water in #2

·     Air sucked into pleural space

No water in #3

·     No suction – device open to ambient air

 

Bronchopleural fistula:

Problems with IPPV

·     Ineffective ventilation

·     Tension pneumothorax

·     Contamination (?)

Overall goals

·     Drainage

·     Isolation

·     Closure

Anaesthetic

·     Spont vent induction

·     Surgeon scrubbed and ready

·     Alternative: awake intubation
