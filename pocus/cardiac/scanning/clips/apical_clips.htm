Apical views:

 

Structure

Eyeball

·      Chamber size

·      Wall thickness

·      Interatrial septum
(often confused with coronary sinus)

·      Pericardial space (near field)

Measurements

·      Atria: ESA, ESV

·      LV cavity: EDA/ESA

·      LV wall thickness

·      RV cavity: EDD/ESD (TV tip level), EDA/ESA

·      RV wall thickness (?)

·      Interatrial septum colour doppler (Nyquist 40cm/s)

Function

Eyeball

·      LV function

·      RV function

LV systolic fx

·      MAPSE (M mode at septal mitral annulus)

·      s’ (PW TDI at septal mitral annulus)

·      LVOT VTI (colour + PWD 0.5cm behind AV)

·      Simpson’s biplane

LV diastolic fx

·      E&A waves (PWD mitral inflow at leaflet tips)

·      e’ (PW TDI septal and lateral mitral annulus)

·      S&D waves (PWED right upper pulmonary vein)

RV systolic fx

·      TAPSE – M mode at lateral tricuspid annulus

·      s’ – (PW TDI at lateral tricuspid annulus)

·      PA pressure – CWD through TR jet

Valves

Eyeball

·      MV

·      TV

·      LVOT/AV

Doppler

·      MV: colour

·      TV: colour

·      LVOT/AV: colour + CWD (max velocity)
