Patient

·     Almost left lateral (moves heart towards probe)

·     Left arm up (opens interspaces)

Probe

AV valve view:

·     Left sternal edge

·     3rd interspace for most images, higher space for AV and RV outflow views

·     Start with PLAX, with AV mid-screen

·     Rotate probe clockwise until dot points to left shoulder

Other views:

·     RV inflow: slide to higher space or angle cephalad

·     All others: slide to lower space or angle caudad

Goals

·     Not sure

·     Mid-pap view: LV cavity circular, see papillary muscles but not mitral valve

Corrections

·     If LV is a side-lying oval: rotate either direction

·     If LV is an upright oval: tilt either direction
