About POCUS

 

Background:

·        Ultrasound machines are everywhere

·        Comorbid cardiovascular disease is common and sometimes undiagnosed

·        Peri-operative cardiovascular insults are common, at times deadly, not always easy to diagnose, not always easy to quantify and require specific treatment

·        Inpatient TTE is hard to get

·        I grew sick and tired of not being able to do echo myself

 

What I envision:

·        Every anaesthetist in Australia learns to do a goal-directed TTE

·        A few anaesthetists per department learn to do a comprehensive TTE

 

What I plan:

·        A teaching course comprising:

o   Knowledge (i.e. ultrasound, anatomy, physiology - online)

o   Interpretation (especially pattern recognition - online)

o   Scanning (in person, supervised, regular)

·        Those who pass the course can breeze through formal qualifications if they wish

·        Ideally the course will be incorporated into the ANZCA curriculum, like EMAC
