Other management of ARDS:

1. Lung-protective

    ventilation

a.Prevent volutrauma:

·          VT <6mL/kg

b.Prevent barotrauma:

·          PPlat <30cmH2O

·          ∆P <15cmH2O

c.Prevent atelectrauma:

·          PEEP 5-15cmH2O

·          Recruitment manoeuvres – good in theatre, bad in ARDS

d.Prevent aspiration:

·          Head up 45°

·          Cuff inflated

·          Suction mouth, pharynx, trachea

·          Chlorhex wash

 

Peri-op benefits:

·          ↓Inflammatory markers

·          ↓Failed extubation

·          ↓Pneumonia

2. Positioning

·          Mild: head up 45°

·          Severe: prone 16/24

3. Medication

Systemic:

·          Relaxant (controversial)

·          Diuretics / restrictive fluid strategy

Inhaled:

·          Inhaled prostacyclin

·          Inhaled nitric oxide

4. Hail Mary

·          vvECMO
