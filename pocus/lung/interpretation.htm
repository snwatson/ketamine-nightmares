Lung ultrasound – interpretation

 

 

Pneumothorax

·      Seen in non-dependent areas

·      Lung point present (gold standard)

·      Clearer A lines

·      No B lines

·      No sliding

·      No sea shore sign

 

 

 

Effusion

·      Seen in dependent areas, easiest with patient upright

·      Hypoechoic collection

·      Collapsed lung swimming about

 

Oedema

·      More than three B lines. Like disco lights.

 

Pneumonia

 

·      Hepatisation (i.e. looks solid)

·      Air bronchograms (i.e. white branches)

·      If early pneumonia: localised patch of B lines, subpleural densities
