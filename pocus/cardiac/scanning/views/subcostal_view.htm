Reference

Video link

08. Subcostal Views - YouTube

Patient

·     Supine

·     Knees up (relaxes abdo wall)

·     ±Breathe in (moves heart towards probe)

Probe

·     Just below xiphoid, slightly to patient’s right side

·     Dot to patient’s left

·     Aim for left shoulder with shallow angle

·     4C: dot to patient’s left, aim for left shoulder, shallow angle

·     SC: rotate 90° anticlockwise, dot to patient’s head

·     IVC: dot to patient’s head, aim straight for the guts and a bit to the right

IVC view

·     Start with S4C view, with RA in middle of screen

·     Rotate anticlockwise 90° while keeping RA in middle of screen

·     Rock probe until tail points to the ceiling

·     Alternative: aim for the spine, dot to the head, slide to patient’s right
