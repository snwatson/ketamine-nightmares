Knobs:

Adjustment

·      Depth: adjusts wave frequency hence both depth and resolution

·      Zoom: just makes the pixels bigger

·      Gain: makes image brighter

·      Focus: send more ultrasound waves to a specified depth

·      Time gain compo: make images brighter at a specified depth

·      Sampling rate: adjusts colour doppler frequency to the jet velocity

Acquisition

·      Acquire: save one cardiac cycle

·      Freeze: stop to allow measurement (real time or saved loop)

·      Measure: calipers for measurement

·      Review: pull that up, Jamie

Modes

·      M mode: motion of every structure along this line

·      2D mode: normal picture

·      Colour doppler: flashing lights

·      CW doppler: highest velocity along this line

·      PW doppler: velocity right here
