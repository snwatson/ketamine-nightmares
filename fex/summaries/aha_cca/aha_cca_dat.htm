2016 Dual antiplatelet therapy

 

Main thing

·         Stent thrombosis vs major bleeding

Emergency (ACS)

·         DES: 12 months

·         BMS: 12 months

·         No stent: 2 weeks (ideal 12 months)

Elective

·         DES: 6 months

·         BMS: 1 month

Qualifiers

·         Duration can be extended if

o    High DAPT score

o    Low bleeding risk

·         If clopidogrel doesn’t work, use ticagrelor

·         If not stenting, use ticagrelor

 

If planning surgery:

Angioplasty

·      Wait 2/52

Bare metal stent

·      DAPT ideal 6/52

Drug-eluting stent

·      DAPT absolute minimum 3 months

·      Duration dependent on patient, lesion, stent, surgery

·      Discussion: surgeon + cardiologist

Recent AMI

·      Highest risk period <2/12

·      Baseline risk after 6-12/12
