Chronic methamphetamine use

 

Complications of use:

Cardiomyopathy

·      Non-ischaemic

·      Often diagnosed and untreated (minimal health contact)

·      Difficult assessment of volume status

·      Difficult titration of crystalloid and blood

·      Risk of APO

Liver disease

·      If Child-Pugh B/C cirrhosis: ↑↑peri-op mortality

·      Risk of needle-stick injury to staff

Poor dentition

·      Risk of damage during intubation

Difficult IV access

·      Ultrasound always

·      ±CVC for major surgery

·      ±IO for resuscitation

 

Intoxication from acute use:

Cardiovascular

·      Risk of arrhythmias, AMI, stroke

Respiratory

·      ?

Neurological

·      Risk of seizures

·      ↑ICP

·      ↑↑MAC: use BIS, avoid relaxant

Logistics

·      Unreliable historian

·      Unknown fasting status

·      Inability to consent for procedure

Management

·      Consider code grey

·      Premed: midazolam IV 0.075mg/kg vs PO 20mg

·      Induction: RSI

·      Analgesic: clonidine 1-2mcg/kg (anxiolytic)

·      Anti-emetic: droperidol 10mcg/kg (anti-psychotic, noting low dose)

 

Withdrawal from chronic use:

Problems

·      ↑SNS -> vasoconstriction

·      ↓↓MAC: use BIS, expect slow emergence

Management

·      Addiction medicine service consult

·      Anxiolytic: clonidine 1-2mcg/kg TDS
