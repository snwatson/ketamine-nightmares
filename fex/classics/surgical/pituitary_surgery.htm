Pituitary surgery

 

Intra-op issues:

Pituitary tumour

·     Mass effects: hypothalamus, pituitary, vision

·     Metastases: rarely relevant

·     Medications: hormone replacement

·     Metabolic: macro vs microadenoma

FESS

Airway:

·     Problem: remote, shared, bloody

·     Solution: oral RAE, throat pack, suction at end

Bleeding:

·     Surgeon: vasoconstrictor -> ↑BP, ↑HR, arrhythmias

·     Anaesthesia: head up, ↓Paw, ↓CO2, ↓HR, ↓BP, smooth emergence

Head up:

·     Cerebral hypoperfusion = A-line + metaraminol infusion

Neuro

·     Require immobility = remifentanil ± paralysis

·     Require smooth emergence without CPAP (dural breach)

·     Vessel injury = massive bleeding + tight brain

 

Post-op issues:

Endocrine dysfunction

·     Central DI in 40%

·     SIADH (delayed)

·     Adrenal insufficiency

Surgical complications

Of dural breach:

·     CSF leak

·     Pneumocephalus (CPAP relatively contraindicated)

·     Meningitis

Other:

·     Bleeding

·     Stroke

·     Hypothalamic injury
