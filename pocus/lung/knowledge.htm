Lung ultrasound – knowledge

 

Normal picture

·      Chest wall: muscle, fat & bone appear the same as elsewhere

·      Pleura: thick white line, shimmering as lung slides past

·      Lung: snow storm due to reflection by air

 

A lines

·      Equidistant curved white lines

·      Represents reverberation between probe and pleura

·      Occurs due to a large difference in density between pleura and lung

·      Normal finding

 

B lines

·      Looks as if someone is waving a torch about

·      Due to subpleural thickening

·      Normal if <3 in the whole field

 

Sliding

·      Normal shimmering appearance of pleura as lung slides past

 

Sea shore sign

·      Normal appearance in M mode

·      Chest wall is still -> looks like waves

·      Lung is moving -> looks like sand
