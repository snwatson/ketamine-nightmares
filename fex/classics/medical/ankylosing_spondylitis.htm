Ankylosing spondylitis

 

Issues:

A

·        Stiff neck

·        Atlanto-axial instability (?)

·        Risk of fracture during laryngoscopy

-> Hyperangulated VL vs AFBI

B

·        Non-compliant chest wall

·        Pulmonary fibrosis

-> ↑RR, ↓VT

C

·        Aortic regurgitation

·        Myocarditis

·        Conduction disease

·        Cardiomyopathy

·        Pericardial effusion

-> TTE before major surgery

Other

·        Anaemia of chronic disease

Epidural

·        Fusion

·        Immobility

·        Spondylolisthesis

·        Epidural space obliteration

·        Higher risk of epidural haematoma (ASRA)

-> Avoid

Medications

·        NSAID -> renal impairment

·        DMARD -> marrow suppression, infection risk

·        Glucocorticoid -> toxicity, insufficiency
