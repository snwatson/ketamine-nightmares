Rheumatoid arthritis

 

Issues:

Airway

Atlanto-axial disease:

·         Present in 30%

·         Unstable in 10%

·         Risk spinal cord damage -> quadriplegia

·         Risk vertebral artery damage -> stroke

·         Risk difficult intubation

 

-> Pre-op C-spine imaging if at risk peri-op (e.g. airway, positioning)

-> Video laryngoscope + hyperangulated blade first line

-> Care with positioning for surgery

Systemic disease

·         Present in 50%

·         CVS: pericardial effusion, accelerated CAD, valve disease, failure

-> Pre-op ECG ±TTE ± stress test

-> Intra-op 5 lead ECG ± A-line + vasopressor infusion

·         Resp: restrictive lung disease, pleural effusion

·         Haem: normocytic anaemia, drug-induced marrow depression

-> Pre-op plt + coag if neuraxial

·         Liver: hepatomegaly, abnormal serum proteins

·         Kidney: drug-induced CKD in 25%

·         Neuro: peripheral nerve compressive neuropathy

-> Beware neuraxial and regional; document neuro status

·         Immune: Sjogren’s, Felty’s

Drugs

·         Glucocorticoids -> risk insufficiency

-> Stress steroid dosing

·         Immunosuppressants

·         DMARDS

·         NSAIDs
