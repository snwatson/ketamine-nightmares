Apical views:

 

How to:

Patient

·     Almost left lateral (moves heart towards chest wall)

·     Left arm up (opens interspaces)

Probe

·     5th interspace, mid-clavicular line

·     A4C: dot points to left side

·     A5C: shallower angle

·     A2C: rotate 30-60° anticlockwise until RV disappears and dot towards axilla

·     A3C: rotate further anticlockwise until dot towards right shoulder

·     Dot points towards right shoulder

Goals

·     Default: apex at top of screen, LV cavity mid-screen

·     For LV: tilt until as long as possible (can cut off atria)

·     For RV: slide medial until RV ‘apex’ at top of screen

·     For atria: tilt until as long as possible (ok to foreshorten ventricles)

·     A5C: see as much of aorta as possible (slide medial and lateral)
