Massive transfusion - simplified

 

Indications for MTP:

By volume

·     50% BV in 4h

·     100% BV in 24h

·     >150mL/min

By scenario

·     Anticipate bleeding +++

·     Thorax, abdo, long bone trauma

·     Obstetric bleeding +++

·     GI bleeding +++

 

Goals:

1.Haemostasis

a. Surgery:

·     Temporising: tourniquets, packs, binders

·     Definitive: not my job

b. Coagulation:

·     Reversal: e.g. protamine

·     Substrate: FFP, platelets, cryo

·     Adjuncts: TXA stat, Ca with each round

·     Heat: air warmer + fluid warmer

c. Permissive hypotension:

·     mAP ≥50 until surgical control

 

Targets: plt >50, INR <1.5, fib >1, T >35, iCa >1.1

Use viscoelastic if available

2.Resuscitation

a. Anaesthesia:

·     Air goes in and out

·     Blood goes round and round

·     Unconscious and immobile

b. Vascular access:

·     2 x IVC + pump set + rapid warmer

·     A-line

·     CVC later

c. Transfusion:

·     CSL 2L max

·     O-neg if urgent

·     Crossmatched if time

·     Cell salvage if available

·     1:1:1 ratio (4 red, ?4?2 yellow, 1 white)

 

Targets: Hb >80, PaO2 >100, pH >7.2, lactate <4
