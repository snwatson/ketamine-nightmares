Primary survey:

Questions

·      Threat to life?

·      Threat to limb?

·      Threat to organ?

Airway

·      Is the C-spine injured?

·      Is the airway injured?

o  Burn?

o  Sharp?

o  Blunt?

·      Is it patent?

·      Is it protected? (assume unfasted)

Breathing

·      Is it injured?

o  Open?

o  Pneumo?

o  Haemo?

o  Flail?

o  Diaphragm?

·      Is oxygenation ok?

o  Colour

o  SpO2

·      Is ventilation ok?

o  Look (both sides)

o  Listen (both sides)

o  Capno

Circulation

·      Is there shock?

o  Brain: GCS

o  Kidneys: urine

o  Skin: colour, CRT

·      If shock, what is the cause?

o  External bleeding?

o  Internal bleeding? (E-FAST*)

o  Tension pneumothorax?

o  Tamponade?

o  Neurogenic?

·      If bleeding, how much?

o  Responding: <20%

o  Transiently responding: 20-40%

o  Not responding: >40%

Disability

·      Is there a head injury?

o  GCS

o  Pupils

o  Lateralising signs

Exposure

·      Are there any injuries we can’t see?

·      Is temperature ok?

*E-FAST: bilat anterior thorax + pericardium + perihepatic + perisplenic + pelvic

 

Primary response:

Goals

·      Fix problems in order of priority

·      Don’t make existing problems worse (esp C-spine and brain)

Team leader

·      Co-ordinate resuscitation

·      Arrange theatre

Surgery

·      Thorax: chest tubes 30Fr Fr 5th ICS MAL

·      Abdomen: packing

·      Pelvis: binder

·      Limbs: reduce and splint

·      (Head -> EVD in theatre)

Airway & breathing

·      Observe

·      Auscultate

·      Manual in-line stabilisation

·      ±Jaw support

·      ±Bag & mask

·      ±Intubate

Circulation

3a. Access:

·      2 x big PIVC

·      Pumpset + fluid warmer

·      Arterial line

·      Urinary catheter

3b. Blood:

·      Activate MTP

·      Take samples

·      Give crystalloid max 2L

·      Give red cells

·      Give clotting products

·      Give adjuncts (TXA, calcium)

·      Keep warm

Disabilty (neuro)

·      Minimise ICP:

o  Physical

o  Physiological

o  Pharmacological

o  Surgical

·      Optimise mAP: 80-90

o  Volume

o  Pressor (careful)

Exposure

·      Blanket

·      Fluid warmer
