2001B09 What do you understand by the term “clearance”?
Using propofol as an example, explain briefly the importance of clearance.

 

List:

·     Intro

·     Significance

·     Drug comparison

 

Intro:

Definition

·   Volume of plasma from which drug is completely removed per unit time

Units

·   mL/kg/min

Equations

·   Clearance = VD x k = VD/τ

·   Total body clearance  = Cl (organ 1) + Cl (organ 2) etc.

Graph

·   Clearance = dose/(AUC concentration vs time)

 

Significance:

Important for

Calculating steady state infusion rate

·   CSS (mg/mL) = infusion rate (mg/min) / clearance mL/mg/min)

Estimating time to offset after long infusion

·   τ = VD / clearance
(where τ  = t1/2 / loge2)

Hence determining suitability for long infusion

Irrelevant to

Calculating bolus dose

·   Dose = Cpt x VDC

Estimating time to offset after bolus

·   Dependent on distribution rate
