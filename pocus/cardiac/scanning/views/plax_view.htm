Patient

·     Almost left lateral (move heart towards probe)

·     Left arm up (opens interspaces)

Probe

Standard view:

·     Left sternal edge

·     3rd interspace

·     Dot towards right shoulder

Other views:

·     RVIT: lower interspace, tilt away from left shoulder, ±rotate anticlockwise

·     RV outflow: higher interspace, tilt towards left shoulder, ±rotate clockwise

·     For aorta: higher interspace, rock towards head

Goals

·     Wide open LV cavity (apex not visible)

·     Ventricular walls horizontal

·     MV leaflets in middle of screen

·     AV leaflets meeting symmetrically in middle of lumen

Corrections

·     If LV not open: rotate until parallel to the long axis; then tilt either way

·     If LV walls not horizontal: slide to higher interspace

·     If apex in view: rock or slide towards sternum

·     If AV cusps not symmetric: rotate or tilt in either direction
