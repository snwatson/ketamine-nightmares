Parasternal short axis:

 

How to:

Patient

·     Almost left lateral (most heart towards chest wall)

·     Left arm up (opens interspaces)

Probe

·     Left sternal edge

·     3rd interspace for most images, higher space for AV and RV outflow views

·     Start with PLAX, with AV mid-screen

·     Rotate probe clockwise until dot points to left shoulder

·     Angle cephalad for RV inflow view

·     Angle caudad for mid-pap, MV and apex views
