Gastric ultrasound – interpretation

Effect of position:

Supine

·     If the stomach looks full, it is full

·     If the stomach looks empty, it might not be
(contents fall from antrum to fundus due to gravity)

Right lateral

·     If the stomach looks full, it is full

·     If the stomach looks empty, it is empty
