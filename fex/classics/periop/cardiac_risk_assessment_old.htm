Cardiac risk assessment – the old way

 

Rule of thumb: order a stress TTE if you would do so in absence of surgery

 

List:

·         Define functional capacity

·         Identify patient risk factors

·         Identify surgical risk factors

·         Calculate risk

·         Decide how to proceed

 

1.Define functional capacity:

1 MET

·      Self-care

4 METs

·      One flight of stairs

·      6km/h walk

4-10 METs

·      Two flights of stairs

·      Heavy housework

>10 METs

·      Sports

 

2.Identify patient risk factors:

Global

·      Elderly

·      ASA ++

·      Poor functional status

Specific

·      IHD

·      Heart failure

·      CVD

·      T2DM insulin

·      CKD

·      (AF)

·      (Obesity)

 

3.Identify surgical risk factors:

Low

·      Day case

·      Endoscopy

·      Superficial

·      Cataracts

·      Breast

Mod

·      Carotid endarterectomy

·      Head and neck

·      Intraperitoneal

·      Intrathoracic

·      Orthopaedic

·      Prostate

High

·      Aortic surgery

·      Major vascular surgery

 

4.Calculate risk:

Scoring:

Low risk: <1% MACE

Mod risk: 1-5% MACE

High risk: >5% MACE

RCRI

·      Major surgery

·      IHD

·      CVD

·      T2DM insulin

·      CKD

NSQIP

·      Major surgery

·      ASA

·      Elderly

·      Functional status

·      CKD

ACS-SRC

·      Complex online tool

MICA

·      Simple version of NSQIP

·  Emergency

Crack on
