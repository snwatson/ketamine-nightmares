Intro:

Action potential

·   Ion channels cycle through resting -> activated -> inactivated states

Determinants of ion flux

·   Channel permeability: voltage dependent

·   Electrochemical gradients

Definitions:

Threshold

·   Membrane potential at which a self-propagating action potential is triggered

Excitability

·   % of Na+ channels returned from inactivated to resting state

·   ∝ rate of rise phase 0

Irritability

·   Closeness to threshold
