Respiratory function tests

 

FER

·      Obstructive: <0.7

·      Restrictive: >0.7 with ↓FVC

Obstruction

Severity = FEV1:

·      Mild: >70%

·      Mod: 50-70%

·      Sev: 30-50%

·      Very sev:<30%

Cause:

·      Asthma = reversible (↑FEV1 >12% or 200mL)

Other:

·      ↓PEFR

·      ↓FEF25-75

Restriction

Severity = FVC:

·      >Mild: >70%

·      Mod: 50-70%

·      Sev: 30-50%

·      Very sev: <30%

Cause:

·      If ↓DLCO: pulmonary

·      If ↔DLCO: neuro, muscular, skeletal

Transfer

Causes of ↓DLCO:

·      ↓Surface area: low lung volume

·      ↓Diffusability: interstitial disease, oedema, emphysema, PHTN

·      ↓Uptake: anaemia

 

*Just for ease of remembering
